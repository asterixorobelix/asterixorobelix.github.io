Catapult

Marketing automation for indie app developers

macOS Desktop App

Find where your app's audience is talking, generate authentic community-aware copy, and post it directly — all from one screen. Built for solo developers who'd rather ship features than spend hours on marketing.

Trend Discovery

Scan Reddit, X, and Hacker News for conversations relevant to your app. Surface threads where your app is a genuine answer to someone's question.

AI-Powered Copy

Generate community-aware responses that match each platform's tone. Powered by your local AI CLI — no API keys to manage, no vendor lock-in.

One-Click Posting

Post directly to Reddit and X from Catapult. Authenticate once, then engage with your audience without switching between browser tabs.

Multi-App Management

Register all your iOS and Android apps in one place. Track marketing efforts across your entire portfolio from a single dashboard.

How It Works

1

Register Your Apps

Add your iOS and Android apps with their store URLs. Catapult builds a profile of what your app does and who it's for.

2

Connect Your Accounts

Authenticate with Reddit and X using OAuth. Your credentials stay on your machine — Catapult never sees your passwords.

3

Discover Opportunities

Catapult scans social platforms for threads where your app is relevant. No spam — only conversations where you can genuinely help.

4

Generate and Post

AI generates a response that fits the community's tone. Review it, edit if you want, and post with one click.

Supported Platforms

Reddit X / Twitter Hacker News Product Hunt

Privacy First

  • All data stored locally on your Mac — nothing leaves your machine
  • OAuth tokens stored in macOS Keychain
  • AI processing via your local CLI — no cloud API calls
  • No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry
  • Open source
