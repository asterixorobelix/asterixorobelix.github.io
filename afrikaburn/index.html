AfrikaBurn Companion

AfrikaBurn 2026

Welcome Home, Burner

Dust, dreams, and radical self-reliance. The companion app for AfrikaBurn in the Tankwa Karoo -- offline maps, art installations, theme camps, events, and everything you need when there is no signal.

Location unlock

Map and project information for the current year are hidden until you arrive at the event. Once the app detects you are near the Tankwa Karoo, all content unlocks automatically -- no action needed on your part.

How it works

1. Download before you go

Install the app while you still have signal. All event data is bundled -- no internet needed at the Burn.

2. Explore the Binnekring

Browse art, theme camps, events, mutant vehicles, and performances. Search, filter, and find what speaks to you.

3. Navigate the dust

Use the offline map to find your way. Mark your camp, locate art, and never get lost in the Tankwa.

Built for the Tankwa

Offline maps

Full map of the Binnekring with theme camps, artworks, toilets, and services. Works without signal.

All projects in your pocket

Art installations, performances, events, theme camps, mutant vehicles, and mobile art. Searchable and filterable.

Works offline

No internet required. All event data is bundled in the app. Download once, use all week in the desert.

Mark your camp

Long-press the map to drop a pin at your camp. Always find your way back through the dust.

Family friendly filter

Filter theme camps and events to show only child-friendly options. Plan safe adventures for the whole family.

Directions and GPS

Travel times from Cape Town, Ceres, and Calvinia. GPS coordinates and offline route guidance.

2026 Theme

Through The Prism

The 2026 theme invites you to explore identity, perception, and presence through the metaphor of light refraction. Like a prism bending light, our identities are multifaceted -- not a single story but many intersecting aspects. Drawing from the African principle "Umuntu Ngumuntu Ngabantu" (a person is a person through other people), we are defined through relationship and community.
