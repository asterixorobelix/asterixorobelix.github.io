Real-time train updates from fellow commuters

Stay informed about Cape Town's Metrorail Southern Line with crowdsourced service updates. Know before you go whether your train is running on time, delayed, or cancelled.

Real-Time Reports

Get instant updates from commuters at stations along your route. See delays, cancellations, and service disruptions as they happen.

Train Schedules

Access complete timetables for the Southern Line. Works offline so you always have schedule information, even without signal.

Save Your Commutes

Save your regular routes for quick access. Get personalized updates for the stations that matter to you.

Community Powered

Help fellow commuters by submitting reports from your station. Build a network of trusted information.
