§ 01 — Features

Built for the dust.

Download it with signal, trust it without. Every feature is engineered for a week in the desert — where the phone towers end and the art begins.

01

Offline maps of the whole Binnekring.

Camps, artworks, toilets, ice, medics — all bundled. Works with no signal, no data, no drama.

02

Every registered project in your pocket.

Art, performances, theme camps, mutant vehicles — searchable, filterable, browsable on the couch or in the dust.

03

Mark your camp.

Long-press anywhere to drop a pin. Find your way home through the dust.

04

Family-friendly filter.

Toggle on to only show child-safe camps and events. Plan adventures for the whole crew.

05

GPS + travel times.

Directions from Cape Town, Ceres and Calvinia, with fuel-stop intel and offline route guidance.

06

Unlocks when you arrive.

Map and project info stay hidden until the app detects the Tankwa — then everything opens, automatically. No action needed. It's a small ritual: sign into the desert, and the guide appears.
